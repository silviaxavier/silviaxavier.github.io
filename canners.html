CANNING STORIES IN NEW YORK CITY

Canners walk the streets of New York collecting cans and bottles to be redeemed, which means to exchange them for five cents each, in authorized redemption centers. They often go unnoticed and unheard. Here is an effort to document this system, recording not only quantities, but also interactions and stories.


On 03.16.2016, Eugene collected cans, plastic bottles, and glass bottles.

The data collection happened using a GPS app, which tracked the canners' path. The quantities and types of materials collected were recorded in paper. Then the data exported from the GPS was edited in QGIS to record the locations of collection.

One dataset is complete so far. The other recorded tracks will be updated soon.
